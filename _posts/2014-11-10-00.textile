h3. Спецификация

* 0.1 ohm 1% 2W current sense resistor
* Up to +26V target voltage
* Up to ±3.2A current measurement, with ±0.8mA resolution
* This chip uses I2C 7-bit addresses 0x40, 0x41, 0x44, 0x45, selectable with jumpers
